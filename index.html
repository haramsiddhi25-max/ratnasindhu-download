About the App

RatnaSindhu is a tourism-based Android application designed to help users explore the beautiful Konkan region efficiently. The app provides detailed information about famous beaches, temples, forts, waterfalls, and local attractions. With its multi-language support and smart travel planning features, users can easily navigate and plan their trips. The application also includes hotel listings, local businesses, and an interactive chatbot to enhance user experience.
