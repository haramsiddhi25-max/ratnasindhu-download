About RatnaSindhu

RatnaSindhu is a smart tourism app designed for exploring the beautiful Konkan region. Discover beaches, temples, forts, waterfalls, and plan your trips easily with a modern interface. With multi-language support and integrated chatbot, your travel becomes smarter and easier.
